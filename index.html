Settings

VOICE & LANGUAGE
Live social events always use the instant on-device voice — Polly is used for Ask/Find/Read answers only, so real-time narration is never delayed by the network.
DETECTION TUNING
TRUSTED VIEWER Off
Off by default. Turning this on shares a still frame from your camera (refreshed every few seconds) with anyone who has the link — not full video, and not enabled unless you turn it on yourself. Turn off any time to revoke access instantly.
VOICE COMMANDS
Say "اسأل" or "ask" to describe surroundings · "اقرأ" or "read" to read text · "دلني" or "find" to search · "من هذا" or "who" for face recognition · "شغل الكاميرا" / "start" and "أوقف" / "stop" for the camera. Needs microphone permission once.
CAREGIVER — ENROLL A KNOWN PERSON
Consent model: only deliberately enrolled people are ever matched by "Who is in front of me?". Strangers are never stored. 3–5 clear frontal photos per person give the best accuracy.
